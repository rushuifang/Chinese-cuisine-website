Mooncake

月饼

Ingredients

Mooncake skin: 150 grams of all-purpose flour, 8 grams of custard powder or milk powder. 110 grams of invert syrup, 2 grams of water, 2 grams of cooked peanut oil, 1 gram of salt.
Wuren filling: Peeled and cooked peanuts, cooked sunflower seeds, cooked black and white sesame seeds, cooked walnut kernels, cooked almonds and raisins. 80 grams of white sugar, 50 grams of maltose, 140 grams of cooked glutinous rice flour, 35 grams of oil, 70 grams of cold water, egg yolks.

Cooking Procedures

1. Add water to the invert syrup and stir well. Add oil again. After whipping thoroughly, add the salt and stir well. Sift into the flour, mix well and knead the dough. Cover it with plastic wrap, and relax for more than two hours.
2. All kinds of nuts should be peeled in advance, fried and crushed.
3. Pour the crushed nuts into the container, add white granulated sugar, maltose, cooked flour, cooked oil and cool water, stir evenly.
4. Divide the loose cake crust into 30 grams per piece and knead it round. Wuren stuffing is divided into 50 grams per piece.
5. Take a cake crust, pat it flat. Put it into the palm of your left hand, push the stuffing inwards with your right hand, and at the same time rotate the left hand to push the crust evenly against the stuffing until it wraps the whole filling. Close cake crust and roll them in the flour pile, shake off the excess flour, and put it in the mold.
6. Press down the mooncake crust with your hands evenly, lift the mold and push out the mooncake. Place it in a baking tray lined with tin foil.
7. Preheat the oven at 180 degrees celsius. Take mooncakes out after 5 minutes, let them cool down. Brush them with egg yolk liquid. Put them in the oven again and lower the temperature to 150 degrees. Bake for about 10 minutes until the surface is evenly colored.
8. After the mooncakes are cool, put them in a fresh-keeping bag.
