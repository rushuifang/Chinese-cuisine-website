Turnip Cake

萝卜糕

Ingredients

Rice 300g, warm water 300g, sweet potato powder 50g, white radish 900g, oil 1 tablespoon, salt 1/2 teaspoon, black pepper powder 2 teaspoons, water 700g.

Cooking Procedures

1. Soak rice for 2 hours and then drain them. Add 300 grams of water to make a rice slurry. Add sweet potato powder, mix well at high speed, pour into a container and set aside.
2. Cut the white radish into small pieces, then put it in a pot and beat it with high speed. Cook the radish at a temperature of 100 degrees celsius. Then add seasoning and cover the mixing bowl.
3. After the temperature drops to below 70 degrees celsius, mix the beaten rice milk and radish puree slowly. Then pour into 8-inch model with rice cake paper or baking paper.
4. After cooking for about 60 minutes, enjoy it right away or cool them down and slice them into small pieces afterwards.
