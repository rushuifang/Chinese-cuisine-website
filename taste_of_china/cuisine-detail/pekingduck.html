Peking Duck

北京烤鸭

Ingredients

1 Peking duck, sugar water, onion, sweet noodle sauce, rice wrap.

Cooking Procedures

1. Get rid of duck feet and tongue. Blow air under the duck skin.
2. Take out the internal organs and use sorghum rods to support it between the spine and the three forks of the breast.
3. Irrigate the water from the knife edge and release it from the anus repeatedly until it is cleaned.
4. Put the iron hook diagonally on the neck and hang the duck.
5. Pouring the skin with boiling water to make the skin smooth and tight.
6. Soak the body with sugar water.
7. Blow the duck dry.
8. Clog the duck's anus. Pour into boiling water, put duck into the oven, roast until the color is ripe.
9. Cut the duck into slices, eat it with the scallion and sweet sauce, and roll it into the rice wrap.
