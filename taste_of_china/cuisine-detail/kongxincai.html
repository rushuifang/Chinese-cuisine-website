Water Spinach

空心菜

Ingredients

Water spinach, chicken essence, garlic, salt.

Cooking Procedures

1. Take one leaf of the spinach in one section and remove it by hand.
2. Wash and drain the water.
3. Chop the garlic head into minced garlic.
4. Heat the oil in a wok. Pour the spinach and stir-fry until it is soft. Season with salted chicken essence.
