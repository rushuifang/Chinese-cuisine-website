Vinegar Potato Shreds

醋熘土豆丝

Ingredients

1500 grams of potatoes, 35 grams of soybean oil, 35 grams of vinegar, 7 grams of refined salt, 10 grams of pepper and green onions, 15 grams of shredded ginger and garlic slices.

Cooking Procedures

1. Peel and wash the potatoes, cut them into filaments and wash away the starch.
2. Spread a large spoon of oil and cook on a high heat.
3. First fry the peppercorns, remove them once it's cooked. Then add onion, shredded ginger, and garlic slices to fry.
4. Add potatoes with vinegar and cook for 10 minutes.
