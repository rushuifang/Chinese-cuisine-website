Sautéed Spare Ribs with Cumin

孜然排骨

Ingredients

Pork ribs, 2 anises, 2 peppercorns, 4 grass fruits, 2 leafs, 2 fennel, 1 tablespoon of dried red pepper, cumin, cooking wine, 2 tablespoon of salt.

Cooking Procedures

1. Wash the ribs, use hot water and ginger slices to scald the ribs and remove the blood. Add some salt, add cooking wine in to pressure cooker.
2. After the pressure cooker makes a noise, turn it off after a while and then remove the ribs.
3. Put hot red pepper in the hot oil pan.
4. Add the ribs and fry them slightly. Remember to stir fry them from time to time. When they are deep fried, sautéed them with cumin grains.
