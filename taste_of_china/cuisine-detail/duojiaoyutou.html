Steamed Fish Head

剁椒鱼头

Ingredients

Reservoir sesame big fish head, red and green chopped pepper, salt, ginger slices, coriander, green onion, ginger, garlic powder, sugar, oil, steamed fish sauce oil.

Cooking Procedures

1. Prepare a big sesame fish head from the reservoir. Cut it into halves after handling it cleanly. Put some ginger slices and parsley on the bottom of the plate to remove fishy smell.
2. Put the processed fish on a plate and marinate with cooking wine, salt, pepper, green onion, ginger and garlic powder for 15 minutes.
3. Next prepare the auxiliary soup for steamed fish: red and green chopped pepper, minced ginger, minced garlic, green onion and sugar.
4. Pour the marinated fish into the soup. Spread the chopped peppers separately, sprinkle with ginger, garlic, green onion, a little sugar, and put it in a steamer boiled water for 10 minutes.
5. Drizzle some steamed fish sauce oil, re-sprinkle with fresh chopped green onion, pour a little oil in the pot, heat it. The fish head is soft and glutinous, fat but not greasy, salty and slightly spicy.
