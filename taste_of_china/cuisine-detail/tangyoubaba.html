Sugar Oil Cake

糖油粑粑

Ingredients

Corn rice cake, brown sugar, white sugar, and honey.

Cooking Procedures

1. Pour oil in the pan and put in rice cakes.
2. Fry rice cakes on both sides.
3. Mix brown sugar, white sugar and honey with water.
4. Pour the fried rice cakes into sugar water, concentrate the sugar over low heat until they hang evenly on the rice cakes.
5. Collect the remaining sugar juice and pour them on top of your cooked rice cakes.
