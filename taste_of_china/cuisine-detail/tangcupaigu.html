Sweet and Sour Ribs

糖醋排骨

Ingredients

Pork chop, shredded ginger, shredded onion, peppercorns, garlic slices, soy sauce, soy sauce, sugar, vinegar, chicken essence, salt, cooking oil.

Cooking Procedures

1. Cut ribs into rows. Bring water to a boil. After boiling, add ribs and boil water for 2-3 minutes. Wash the foam, drain and set aside.
2. Add fresh soy sauce, a little dark soy sauce. Add onion ginger, garlic slices, mix well, together with the drained ribs. Cover with plastic wrap and marinate for a few minutes.
3. While marinating, pour vinegar and an appropriate amount of granulated sugar into a small bowl according to your taste, and adjust the ratio of sweetness and sourness.
4. Add a few peppers when cooking oil is heated over medium heat. Use chopsticks to put the ribs to the heated pot. Fry the ribs are on medium heat until golden on both sides, pour the sauce of pickled ribs and pour in the sweet and sour sauce. Stir constanly.
5. Add a small amount of water. After the it is boiled, turn to a low heat and continue to simmer for about 10 minutes.
