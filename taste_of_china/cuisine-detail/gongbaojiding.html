Kung Pao Chicken

宫保鸡丁

Ingredients

Chicken breast 200g, peanut kernel 100g, sugar 7g, soy sauce 10ml, water starch 10ml, pepper 3g, ginger 3g, garlic 3g, green onion 20g, cooking wine 5g, salt 5g, dried chili 20g.

Cooking Procedures

1. Wash and dry the chicken breasts, cut them into small cubes of 1.5cm. Mix them in water with starch and soy sauce. Stir well and marinate for 20 minutes. Wash the green onions and cut into 1cm long segments. Cut off the ends of the dried chili and remove the chili seeds.
2. Mix water starch, soy sauce, salt, white sugar and cooking wine in a small bowl, mix well to make seasoning juice.
3. Heat the oil in the hot pot on medium heat. Put the peanut kernels when it is hot. Slowly reduce the oil to a slight color on lower heat. Remove the drained oil and set aside.
4. Continue to heat the oil in the hot pot over medium heat. Put the marinated chicken into it, and stir quickly until loose. Wait for the chicken to be cooked, then remove and drain the oil.
5. Leave the bottom oil in the pot, put the peppers and dried chili peppers in the heat. Fry them on low heat, and then add the green onions, ginger, garlic and chicken. Stir for 10 minutes. After the soup thickens, add peanuts and stir fry for 5 minutes.
