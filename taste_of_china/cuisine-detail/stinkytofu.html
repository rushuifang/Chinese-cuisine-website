Stinky Tofu

臭豆腐

Ingredients

Regular firm tofu, 3 stinky tofu, liquor, mineral water.

Cooking Procedures

1. Cut the regular tofu into pieces.
2. Prepare three pieces of stinky tofu.
3. Emulsify the stinky tofu with a mineral water and stir well.
4. Pour the liquor of high alcohol percentage.
5. Pour the adjusted stinky tofu into the regular tofu.
6. Cover the container and store in a cool place.
7. Ready to eat after 24 hours when they all become stinky.
