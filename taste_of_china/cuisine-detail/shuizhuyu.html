Boiled Fish

水煮鱼

Ingredients

Tilapia fish fillet, soybean sprouts, pepper, dried chili, ginger powder, chicken essence, pepper powder, chili powder, sugar, vinegar, starch, garlic, ginger, green onion, cooking wine, Pixian bean paste, egg white, broth, chives.

Cooking Procedures

1. Slice tilapia flakes ​​into pieces and marinated them with a little salt, cooking wine, ginger powder, starch and an egg white for 15 minutes.
2. Add clean water in a pot, boil it and spread the bean sprouts to control the moisture, and put them in the pot evenly.
3. After the pan is set on a high heat and oiled, add peppercorns and three tablespoons of red oil watercress to saute. Add ginger, garlic, green onion and dry red pepper and stir-fry.
4. Pour the broth in, add salt, cooking wine, pepper, chili powder, a little vinegar, sugar and soy sauce. Keep boiling the soup and put pieces of fish one by one, disperse them with chopsticks, put chicken essence. Wait for 3 to 4 minutes and turn off the heat.
5. Pour the cooked fish and all the soup into the large pot of bean sprouts.
6. Take another clean pot and pour the oil. After the oil is hot, turn off the heat and let it dry for a while. Then add a lot of pepper and dried chili. Slowly stir out the flavor of pepper and chili with a small heat, and pour the oil in the pot with pepper and chili into the large pot of fish and sprinkle with chives.
