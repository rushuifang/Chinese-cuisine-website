Shengjian Bao

生煎包

Ingredients

Bun: 250g of all-purpose flour, 150ml of warm water, 1 tablespoon of baking powder, 2 tablespoon of sugar.
Filling: 180g of minced meat, green onion, and ginger.

Cooking Procedures

1. Dissolve the baking powder and sugar in warm water and mix them well, let them stand for about 10 minutes.
2. Add baking powder water into flour, knead it into a smooth dough, and then put the dough in a warm and damp place.
3. Put green onion, minced ginger, a spoonful of salt, a little sugar, cooking wine, a spoonful of soy sauce together. Slowly mix them to make the filling watery, then add a spoonful of cooking oil.
4. Take out the dough after it is twice as large, punch out the bubbles, and knead it well.
5. Knead the dough into long strips, and cut it into 16 equal parts. Then roll a small dough into a round piece. wrap the filling, knead it into a bun, and then put the wrinkle face down.
6. Cover the buns with a layer of damp gauze and let them ferment for the second time for 15-20 minutes.
7. Pour the oil in the frying pan, soak the bottom, and cook until the bottom is golden, then add half a bowl of water, put a lid on top of frying pan and cook until the water is almost dry, and finally sprinkle with green onion and sesame.
