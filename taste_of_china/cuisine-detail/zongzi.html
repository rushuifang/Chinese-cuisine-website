Zongzi

粽子

Ingredients

Glutinous rice, zongye, pork, starch, sugar, wine, salt, soy sauce.

Cooking Procedures

1. Boil the rice dumplings before washing them, then soak them. Cut pork into small cubes, add starch, soy sauce, salt, sugar and white wine and marinate for a three hours.
2. Wash and soak glutinous rice. The rice dumplings are folded in half into a funnel shape.
3. Pack dates and peanuts if preferred.
4. After they are wrapped, put them in a large pot and add the water to the zongzi. Boil them on low heat for about two hours.
