Duck Blood in Chili Sauce

毛血旺

Ingredients

Duck blood, soy sprouts, lettuce leaves, Pixian watercress, ginger slices, garlic slices, meat bone soup, dried red pepper, peppercorns, monosodium glutamate, chicken essence, rock sugar, spring onion.

Cooking Procedures

1. Pour the watercress, ginger and garlic of Pixian in a pot over medium heat, stir constantly. Add the broth, then rock sugar, chicken essence, and boil over high heat.
2. Let the lettuce leaves be slightly scalded in the pot and immediately pick them up. Put them in a large bowl to make a base.
3. Add duck blood and soy bean sprouts to the boiled soup. After 15 minutes, put them in a large soup pot, sprinkle with green onion.
4. Heat the salad oil, add peppercorns and chili peppers, and add flavor to it, then just pour them on top of the duck blood.
