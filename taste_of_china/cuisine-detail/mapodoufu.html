Mapo Tofu

麻婆豆腐

Ingredients

300 grams of tofu, 100 grams of pork filling, 1 chive, 1 small piece of ginger, 6 cloves of garlic, 1 tablespoon of peppercorns, proper amount of starch, 2 dried chillies, 30 grams of cooking oil, 1 tablespoon of sesame oil, 1/2 tablespoon of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of bean paste, 2 teaspoons of sugar.

Cooking Procedures

1. Dice tofu, chop shallot, ginger, garlic and dried chili. Set them aside.
2. Add oil to the pan and heat it up. First sauté the minced shallots, minced ginger, minced garlic, minced chili powder and bean paste, then add the pork filling and stir-fry.
3. Add water, boil tofu, soy sauce and sugar for 3 minutes.
4. After thickening with starch, put it in the plate.
5. Heat the sesame oil and sauté the pepper, then pour the pepper oil on the tofu.
