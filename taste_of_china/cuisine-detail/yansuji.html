Salt and Pepper Chicken

盐酥鸡

Ingredients

Boneless chicken breast, Thai basil, one raw egg yolk, sweet potato powder, two minced garlic, salt, liquor, soy sauce, sugar, pepper, five-spice powder.

Cooking Procedures

1. Chop the chicken into small pieces.
2. Marinate chicken: add two tablespoons of white wine, one minced garlic, one teaspoon of five-spice powder, half teaspoon of pepper, soy sauce, and about two tablespoons of sugar.
3. Mix them well by hand, seal and put them in the refrigerator to marinate for one hour.
4. Add an egg yolk to marinated chicken, and mix them well.
5. Coat the chicken piece by piece with sweet potato powder. After all are covered with the powder, let them stand for ten minutes so that the powder will not fall off easily when it is fried.
6. Heat the oil on the pan to about 160 degrees celsius.
7. Put the chicken into the frying pan one by one, you can add the larger pieces first.
8. Don't flip the chicken immediately after it's in the pan, wait for the chicken to float and come into shape. Then flip the chicken to make sure they are golden on both sides.
9. At this time, add the Thai basil in, and the aroma of the basil will come out after frying.
10. Pick up chicken and basil in about 5-10 seconds.
11. Raise the oil temperature, put the chicken and the Thai basil in the oil and fry for another 10 seconds until they become crispy. Remove the chicken and the basil, drain the oil, put chicken in a bowl, add the salt and pepper while hot, mix them well and then serve. If you like it to be spicy, sprinkle the right amount of chili powder.
