Stuffed Tofu

豆腐酿

Ingredients

Tofu 2 large pieces, garlic puree 1 tsp, soy sauce 2 tbsp, ground pork 300g, water 1 cup, sugar 1 teaspoon.

Cooking Procedures

1. Put the ground meat in a large bowl, add soy sauce, stir the garlic puree, add a small amount of water and beat until the ground meat absorbs moisture.
2. Hollow out tofu in the middle and add ground meat.
3. Fry until golden.
4. Pour 2 tablespoons of flavored fully quenched soy sauce in a choke pot. Add water and a little fine sugar.
5. After the water is boiled, the remaining sauce is thinly drizzled on the tofu.
