Shrimp Noodle

虾仁生面

Ingredients

Corrugated rice noodle, 2 eggs, 5 mushrooms, 3 shrimps.

Cooking Procedures

1. Soak the mushrooms and shrimps in hot water for 15 minutes. Cut the mushrooms into strips.
2. Boil mushrooms and shrimps in hot water for 10 minutes to make the umami in the soup more intense.
3. Use non-stick hot pot covered with a little oil. Put the eggs in and add the right amount of salt. Fry the eggs till they are crispy.
4. Add the noodles when the soup is almost cooked. Cook for another 8 to 10 minutes. Turn off the heat, put the fried eggs and shrimps on the noodles.
