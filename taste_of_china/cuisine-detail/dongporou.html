Dongpo Pork

东坡肉

Ingredients

Pork belly, chives, ginger, soy sauce, soy sauce, cooking wine, rock sugar, star anise, pepper, cinnamon, cloves.

Cooking Procedures

1. Wash the large pieces of pork belly in a cold water pot. Add cooking wine to boil, skim off the froth. Cook for 5 minutes and remove pork. Cut them into cubes of about 5 cm.
2. Wash 1 shallot and remove the roots. Peel the ginger and pat the pine. Boil water with 3 anises, pepper, cinnamon, and cloves for 10 minutes.
3. Spread the shallots evenly on the bottom of the casserole. Put the ginger blocks evenly and lay the skins down. Pour the cooking wine, soy sauce, soy sauce, rock sugar. Add warm water and bring it to a boil. Turn to minimum heat, cover and simmer for about two hours. Add salt.
4. Open the lid, turn the meat gently. Turn the skin side up, and cook for another half an hour with a simmering water.
