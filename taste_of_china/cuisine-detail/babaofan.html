Eight Treasure Rice

八宝饭

Ingredients

Red bean paste material: 340 grams of red beans, 3/4 cup sugar, 80ml oil.
Babao Rice Ingredients: 1.5 cups of glutinous rice, appropriate amount of red bean paste, 1 tablespoon of oil, a pinch of salt, appropriate amount of various preserved fruits.
Syrup material: 1 tablespoon of white sugar, 1 tablespoon of honey, half a glass of starch.

Cooking Procedures

Red bean paste:
1. Wash the red beans, soak them in cold water overnight. Then put them in the pot and add water. After the water is boiled, lower the heat to slowly simmer the red beans until they are soft.
2. Drain the cooked red beans, keep the drained water on the side.
3. Put the drained red beans into a food processor, and slowly add the drained water until the red beans become mud. Make sure to join slowly.
4. Pour oil into the pot, pour red bean paste after heating, and stir slowly. Stir-fry until the red beans are dry, add sugar and continue to fry until the sugar melts.
Eight Treasure Rice:
1. Soak glutinous rice in water for at least 4 hours.
2. Drain the water from the soaked glutinous rice, pour it into a boiling steamer and steam for 15-20 minutes.
3. Steamed glutinous rice. Add a tablespoon of oil and a pinch of salt and mix them well.
4. Prepare all kinds of preserved fruits. Add a layer of oil in a large bowl, place the preserved fruits in the bottom of the bowl according to your favorite pattern. Add glutinous rice, and then spread some red bean paste on the glutinous rice.
5. Finally, fill the entire bowl with the remaining glutinous rice, and squeeze it tightly with a spoon. Then steam for another 30 minutes.
6. Start cooking syrup while steaming Babao rice: put sugar, honey and water together in a small pot to boil to the point that the white sugar to melt. Then add water starch and stir to make the solution more viscous, turn off the heat after boiling.
7. The steamed eight-treasure rice comes out and is covered with a plate. Turn it over. After it gets cold, the bowl is opened and poured with the syrup.
