Double Cooked Pork Slices

回锅肉

Ingredients

400g pork belly, 2 green garlic, 1 green pepper, 1 red pepper, 5 ginger slices, Pixian red oil bean paste, 3/2 tablespoons, 1 tablespoon cooking wine, 1 tablespoon soy sauce.

Cooking Procedures

1. Rinse the skinned pork. Remove the hair. Wash it in a cold water pot and cook until it becomes hard.
2. Rinse the cooked pork and let it cool, cut into thin and large pieces.
3. Put one teaspoon of oil in the pot. Add the sliced ​​meat and ginger slices. Fry slowly on low heat until the oil comes out, and the surface color becomes golden.
4. Add 3/2 tbsp of red oil bean paste.
5. Stir fry until red oil comes out. then add a spoonful of soy sauce and stir fry the cooking wine for a while.
6. Add green garlic white, red and green peppers.
7. Stir-fry until the green garlic is white and red.
8. Finally add the green garlic leaves and stir fry for 5 minutes.
