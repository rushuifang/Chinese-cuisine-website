Buddha Jumps Over the Wall

佛跳墙

Ingredients

9 pork ribs, 9 pork tendons, 8 quail eggs, fresh scallops, 5 dry shiitake mushrooms, 6 yam, 5 bamboo shoots, 2 straw mushrooms, 3 carrots, 10g shrimp, 1 tablespoon oyster sauce, half tablespoon rice wine, a few drops of sesame oil, pepper, sugar, sweet potato powder, salt, 1 tablespoon of soy sauce, black vinegar, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 3 star anise, 2 spring onions, 2 slices of ginger.

Cooking Procedures

1. Pork tendons are boiled in water and marinated for about 1 hour; the ribs are marinated in oyster sauce, sugar, rice wine, pepper and sesame oil for about 30 minutes; dried shiitake mushrooms are soaked and cut into thin sections. Wash mushrooms and the bamboo shoots Shred. Peel the whole garlic and remove the film, peel the yam and cut it to a suitable size, and boil the small scallops for 20 seconds.
2. Add oil to the hot pot, wrap the sweet potato powder before frying the ribs, fry the garlic, quail eggs, and ribs until golden brown, then pick them up and reserve.
3. Add 1 spoon of oil to the hot pot, saute the shrimp, garlic, shredded bamboo shoots and mushrooms, fry for about 3 minutes, then add 6-7 bowls of water, with the right amount of salt, 1 spoonful of soy sauce, 2 tablespoons of rice wine, a little black vinegar and a little pepper for the taste. After a large roll, scoop the soup into a large urn, then put the yam, pork tendons, quail eggs, spare ribs, straw mushrooms into the urn.
4. Put the big urn in the electric pot and put the small scallops and carrot slices into the urn.
