炸酱面

Ingredients

Yellow sauce, sweet pasta sauce, onion, green onion, garlic, minced meat, carrots, green peppers, cucumbers.

Cooking Procedures

1. First cut the onion, scallion and garlic into minced pieces.
2. Stir-fry the minced pork until thoroughly cooked.
3. Turn on a small fire and stir yellow sauce in your frying pan.
4. Add sweet paste sauce on low heat, make sure to stir constantly.
5. Add onion, green onion and minced garlic.
6. Stir well over low heat for 2 minutes and turn off the heat.
7. Cucumber shreds, carrot shreds, green onion shreds, green pepper shreds, bean sprouts shreds, etc. should be prepared in advance and can be added freely according to personal taste.
8. Cook noodles in boiling water.
9. Once noodles are cooked, go through them in cold water. Dry them out and add your cooked sauce.
